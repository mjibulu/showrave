02 / Ticket Scanner

Fast entry, even when the venue network is not.

A dedicated operator app for event-day validation, designed for quick decisions at the door and reliable recovery when connectivity is limited.

  • Online and offline QR validation
  • Duplicate protection, admission history, and upload review
  • Account and event-PIN access for event teams

03 / Messaging

  • Live presence, typing states, and message delivery
  • Shared support inbox with attachments and notifications
  • Event-aware organiser and attendee conversations
